How to Find All City of Westminster Relics – Watch Dogs: Legion Map Walkthrough

Map of City of Westminster Borough in Watch Dogs: Legion

“Map overview showing the City of Westminster borough in Watch Dogs: Legion along with relics locations. Zoom in to find precise locations for all relics.”

8. DedSec Bracelet 1/5

Location: Thorne's Felts

Walkthrough: You can easily get this relic without triggering enemies by climbing the orange pipe on the southeast wall of the building. After climbing it, jump to the relic's location and grab it.

9. Masquerade Ball Prop 1/5

Location: Regalia Theatre

Walkthrough: The relic can be found on top of the theatre entrance and there are many ways to reach it. You can hack and drive the lift located on the back or just throw your Spider Bot to get the relic easily.

10. Fake DedSec Business Card

Location: Leicester Square

Walkthrough: All you need to do is jump over the fence and grab the small relic.


11. DedSec Bracelet 2/5

Location: The National Gallery

Walkthrough: The relic is located on the roof of the building and there are many ways to get there. One easy way is to hack the window washing lift on the back. You can also climb a construction area on the left side of the building to reach the roof.


12. Prototype (Optik 0.1) 2/4

Location: St. James's Square

Walkthrough: You can easily find this relic on the north-east side of the park.


13. 17th Century Medical Saw 1/4

Location: Horse Guards Parade

Walkthrough: Throw your Spider Bot on the northern roof (near the cafe) and jump until you reach the relic's area. Enter in one of the two vents (left or right) and find the way to the relic.


14. Scooter Headlamp 2/5

Location: Duck Island Cottage

Walkthrough: Simply climb the walls and fences to enter the small house area. Use your Spider Bot to pass through the small gap and grab the relic.


15. Floppy Disk

Location: Safehouse

Walkthrough: Under the stairs, close to the beer machine.


16. Mobile Phone 1/4

Location: Lambeth Bridge

Walkthrough: Climb down the stairs under the bridge and use the Spider Bot to access the relic.


17. Platform Shoe 1/3

Location: Vauxhall Bridge

Walkthrough: Similar to the Mobile Phone (16): Climb down the stairs under the bridge and grab the relic.
